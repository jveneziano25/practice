CellDash!

Description

CellDash! is a 2D platformer game featuring Mito, a mitochondrion protagonist, navigating through various cell types in the human body. Inspired by the platforming mechanics of "Celeste" and the educational narrative of "Osmosis Jones," players will guide Mito on a journey to restore cellular balance by overcoming obstacles and defeating pathogens.

Levels

  • The Pulse Caverns
  • The Nephron Labyrinth
  • The Detox Chambers
  • The Neural Network
  • The Respiratory Maze

Characters

  • The Protagonist: Mito
  • The Teacher: Dr. Neura
  • The Helper: Erythra
  • The Protector: Lymphie
  • The Advisor: Hepatica
